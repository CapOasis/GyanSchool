Privacy Policy

At GyanSchool, your privacy is important to us—and we mean that. We know how important your personal information is, and we’re committed to being transparent, respectful, and responsible in how we use it. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect your data when you interact with our services—whether you're visiting our website, signing up for a course, or joining our franchise family.

1. What We Collect

We only collect the information we genuinely need to give you the best possible experience. This may include:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Location and education details (when enrolling in a course or expressing franchise interest)
  • Communication history (so we can offer personalized support)
  • Website usage data via cookies (so we can improve our site)

2. Why We Collect It

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Provide you with course, franchise, and service-related updates
  • Share relevant offers and promotions (but only if you want them!)
  • Improve your experience with our platform

3. We Don’t Sell Your Data—Ever

Your personal data is never for sale. We don’t trade, rent, or sell your information to any third party.

4. Third-Party Tools

Sometimes we use trusted tools (like WhatsApp, Google, or Meta ads) to communicate or understand how you’re interacting with our services. These platforms may also collect data as per their privacy policies. We ensure these partners follow strict data protection standards.

5. Your Choices

You’re in control. You can:

  • Ask what data we have about you
  • Request corrections or deletions
  • Opt-out of marketing messages at any time (we won’t take it personally)

To make any request, just drop us an email at info@capoasis.com. We’re happy to help.

6. Keeping Your Data Safe

We take data security seriously. From encryption to internal privacy checks, we do our best to keep your information protected from unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Changes to This Policy

We might update this policy from time to time, and when we do, we’ll let you know. The latest version will always be available on our website.

Let’s Stay Transparent
